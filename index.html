Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a transportation facility?
Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation for students across major routes.

Average ratio is 20:1 to ensure individual attention.
Does the school offer extracurricular activities?
Yes, including sports, arts, music and clubs.
How do I apply for admissions?
Submit the online form and our team will contact you with the next steps.
How is the safety of students ensured?
CCTV, controlled access, trained staff and regular safety drills.
